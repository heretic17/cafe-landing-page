Welcome to Cozy Bean Cafe

Nestled in the heart of the city, Cozy Bean Cafe is your perfect getaway from the hustle and bustle of daily life. Our passion for crafting the finest coffee blends is matched only by our commitment to providing a warm and inviting atmosphere. Whether you're here to savor a freshly brewed cup of coffee, catch up with friends, or simply relax with a good book, Cozy Bean Cafe is your home away from home. Come and experience the cozy comfort and delightful aromas that make us a beloved spot for coffee lovers everywhere.

Cozy Bean Cafe Menu

Coffee

  • Espresso - A rich, full-bodied shot of pure coffee perfection.

  • Americano - Smooth and bold, with hot water added to a shot of espresso.

  • Latte - Creamy steamed milk blended with a shot of espresso.

  • Cappuccino - A harmonious blend of equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and foam.

  • Macchiato - Espresso marked with a dollop of frothy steamed milk.

Specialty Drinks

  • Caramel Macchiato - A sweet and creamy espresso drink with caramel syrup and a drizzle on top.

  • Vanilla Latte - A classic latte infused with the smooth taste of vanilla.

  • Hazelnut Mocha - A delightful mix of espresso, chocolate, and hazelnut syrup.

  • Matcha Latte - A vibrant green tea latte made with finely ground matcha.

  • Pumpkin Spice Latte - Seasonal favorite with pumpkin spice and steamed milk.

Tea & Other Beverages

  • Chai Latte - Spiced black tea blended with steamed milk.

  • Herbal Tea - A selection of calming and invigorating herbal infusions.

  • Hot Chocolate - Rich and creamy hot chocolate, perfect for a cozy treat.

  • Iced Coffee - Refreshing cold coffee served over ice.

  • Smoothies - A variety of fruit and veggie smoothies made fresh.

Pastries & Snacks

  • Croissants - Flaky and buttery, freshly baked.

  • Muffins - A selection of flavors, including blueberry, chocolate chip, and bran.

  • Scones - Classic scones served with clotted cream and jam.

  • Bagels - Freshly baked bagels with cream cheese, butter, or flavored spreads.

  • Cookies - Assorted cookies, including chocolate chip, oatmeal raisin, and sugar.
